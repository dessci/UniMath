Complex Hyperbolic Space

Consider the Hermitian inner product on ℂ n+1 given by 〈 u,v 〉= u * I n,1 v= u * [ I n 0 0 −1 ]v, where u, v∈ ℂ n+1 and u* denotes the conjugate transpose of u. Let ℙ n denote complex projective n-space, the space of complex lines through the origin in ℂ n+1. The action of U(n, 1) on ℂ n+1 induces an action on ℙ n. If A∈U( n, 1 ) then ζA induces the same transformation of ℙ n for any unit complex number ζ. Thus the induced group of projective transformations is PU(n,1)= U(n,1)/ S 1. One easily checks that PU(n,1) acts transitively on ℍ ℂ n .
